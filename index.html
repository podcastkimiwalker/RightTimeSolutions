Follow-Up System

Simple automation plan

The goal is not to over-automate. The goal is to make sure no serious inquiry gets missed and every lead receives a professional response.

1. Lead Confirmation

Send instant email/SMS confirming the inquiry was received and inviting them to book or watch for next steps.

2. Internal Notification

Notify Rashid or the team when a new lead comes in, including service interest and project details.

3. Booking Reminder

Send reminders before consultation calls to reduce no-shows.

4. No-Booking Follow-Up

If someone submits a form but does not schedule, send a short reminder sequence.

5. Post-Session Follow-Up

After project completion, send thank-you message, review request, and optional next-step offer.

6. Retainer Invitation

Invite strong-fit clients into monthly content support once they have experienced the service.
